Main menu:
- You can add a new recipe or create a shopping list using already existing recipes

Create shopping list:
- Click on the recipe whose ingredients you want to include in your shopping list
- Type the number of portions you want to cook for the recipe and click 'Add'
- Repeat for all the recipes you want to include
- You can always empty your Chosen recipes filed by clicking 'Empty'
- When ready, click 'Generate' to create your shopping list
- Your shopping list will get generated below
- You can always edit your shopping list
