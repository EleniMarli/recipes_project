Instructions:
Season beef with salt and pepper, then coat with flour.
Sear beef in butter until browned, then set aside.
Saut√© onions and garlic in the same pan.
Add mushrooms and cook until softened.
Return beef to the pan.
Pour in beef broth, simmer until beef is tender.
Stir in sour cream, heat through.
Adjust seasoning, serve over egg noodles or rice.

Ingredients:
beef sirloin, 680 gr
olive oil, 2 tablespoon(s)
onion, 1 unit(s)
garlic, 2 clove(s)
mushrooms, 225 gr
all-purpose flour, 2 tablespoon(s)
beef broth, 240 ml
Worcestershire sauce, 2 tablespoon(s)
Dijon mustard, 1 tablespoon(s)
sour cream, 120 ml
dry white wine, 60 ml
