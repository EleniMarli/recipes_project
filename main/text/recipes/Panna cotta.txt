Instructions:
Heat cream and sugar until sugar dissolves.
Soften gelatin in cold water, then add to the cream mixture.
Stir until gelatin dissolves.
Add vanilla extract and mix.
Pour into molds and refrigerate until set.
Serve chilled, optionally with berry compote.

Portions:
4

Ingredients:
heavy cream, 2 cup(s)
whole milk, 1 cup(s)
granulated sugar, 0.5 cup(s)
vanilla bean, 1 unit(s)
unflavored gelatin, 1 packet(s)
cold water, 3 tablespoon(s)
