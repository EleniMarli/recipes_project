Instructions:
Slice tomatoes and mozzarella into even rounds.
Arrange tomato, mozzarella, and basil leaves on a plate in alternating layers.
Drizzle with extra virgin olive oil.
Optionally, add a touch of balsamic glaze.
Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Portions:
4

Ingredients:
tomatoes, 4 unit(s)
fresh mozzarella cheese, 450 gr
fresh basil leaves, 1 cup(s)
extra virgin olive oil, 3 tablespoon(s)
